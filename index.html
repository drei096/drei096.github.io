About Me

Hi! I am Aldrey D. Gaurana. I am currently a student at De La Salle University - Manila in the Philippines taking up Bachelor of Science in Interactive Entertainment Technology Major in Game Development. I am currently on my third year with a CGPA of 3.685 and will be expecting to graduate by 2023.

My love for games started all the way from when I was a kid, but I did not know at the time that game development could be a career path. It was only when I was in high school that I first fell in love with programming and decided to pursue a career in game development after I finished my playthrough of a certain game.

Aside from programming, I am also exposed to the intricacies, processes, and life of being in an organization. Currently, I am the Vice President for External Relations at Peer Tutors Society, a student-tutor organization under the Office of the Associate Dean of the College of Computer Studies in the university.

I was also the Vice President for Human Resources at Young Entrepreneurs' Society - DLSU. I had mentored 2 of my junior officers which became the President and the Executive Vice President of the organization after my term.
