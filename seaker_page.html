Currently in development for the capstone project of my college course.

A 3D Roguelite-inspired Hidden Objects game that features various marine environments and creatures found in the Philippines. A serious advocacy game that focuses on educating and informing the Filipino youth on the unique flora and fauna of the Philippine waters, as well as the threats that affect them. This game is being developed by a group of 6 students, including me.
