Games of the Generals

Unity

Developed for my Introduction to Game AI class.

Games of the Generals is a strategy board game from the Philippines wherein two players move pieces representing soldiers of different ranks to capture the enemy's flag or all of their pieces. The rank of the pieces is hidden from each player until they are adjacent to an opponent's piece, in which a third party (the arbiter) will decide which piece gets captured based on rank hierarchy, adding an element of mystery and deception. This game was developed by myself and my class project partner.
